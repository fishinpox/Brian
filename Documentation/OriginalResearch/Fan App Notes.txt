Overall ArchitectureAll apps form one ecosystem under a unified backend with role-based access. Fans get lightweight engagement; creators get productivity + community tools; managers get oversight; agencies get enterprise CRM/HR depth. This merges productivity super-app capabilities (Todoist/TickTick/Notion/etc.) with creator tools (Patreon/Discord/Holodex/VGen/etc.) while reducing parasocial gaps through community emphasis and boundaries.
