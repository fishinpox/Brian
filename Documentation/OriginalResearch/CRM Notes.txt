CRM Notes

CRM's goal is to scale from an individual creator all the way up to a large agency such as hololive or nijisanji and interact within the App.

Huge Database, charge monthly for cloud storage space.

Merge in managers having access to a vtuber's public persona and content release scheduling. Allow managers to schedule/modify/update anything and everything from "Carrd" website features per vtuber.

Each vtuber has internal Milestones and metrics to hit based on the Agency's needs.
The individual Vtuber is typically rewarded for hitting "100,000 followers on Youtube"
And again, rewarded at 1 Million, 2 Million and so on and the Agency "Grants them a Wish" at each million hit per vtuber.  Use stats to track when these will be hit internally. Build in social blade ( https://socialblade.com/ )

The Agency puts on large projects forcing everyone to participate (EnReco)
A Vtuber can request the Agency to put on a large scale event for the company vtubers to participate in such as paying server hosting costs for games like Minecraft.

Agency puts on large events at Convention Centers. Requires company wide participation of creating.

Agency's force their vtubers to preplan and reserve studio time to record future songs, content creation for social media (Youtube Shorts, TikTok) birthday lives for each girls.
